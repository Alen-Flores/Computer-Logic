Binary is Simple

- And only 1 required gate to build all other gates:


\[ \begin{aligned} \lnot A &= \text{NAND}(A,A) \\ A \land B &= \text{NAND}(NAND(A,B),NAND(A,B)) \\ A \lor B &= \text{NAND}(\lnot A, \lnot B) \\ A \oplus B &= (A \lor B) \land \text{NAND}(A,A) \\ \end{aligned} \]
